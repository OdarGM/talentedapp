What's TalentEd?

The project is called TalentEd, an online social media platform with the objective of sharing your talents, users will be able to upload images-photos demonstrating their skills, if another user views any of your uploads they can "talent" you. Talents are the amount of times other users have "talented" an upload of yours. Talenters are the total amount of users that "talented" an upload of yours. (not followers) Talenteds are the amount of talenteds you've given to other users. Followers, those who follow you. Followed, those whom you follow. The Talent Categories for now are: Arts, Music, Performance/Acting, Coding, DIY Items, Cooking, Sports Each user will choose the categories or category when creating their account.
